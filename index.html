About Us

Galz Hostel is one of the best hostel located at Jawlakhel lalitpur. This hostel is providing very good service for peoples who work and study around kupondole area. Hostel have quite good environment with small garden and peaceful environment.

This hostel have served hundreds of college students and gain trust by providing the best service. This hostel have been An awesome place for students for living away from home with secured environment, Good food, Supportive guardians and many more facilities.

Generally peoples who study in Pulchowk campus, Patan campus and other colleges nearby Kupondole stay there. Most of the colleges are nearby this hostel which makes it easy for the students to go to their respective colleges. It's Peaceful Environment to stay in our hostel. It is associated with hostel association of nepal

Rules & Regulation
The student/hosteller should strictly follow hostel's rules and regulations.All the illegal activities are strictly prohibited. Students/hosteller can't smoke and drink alcohol around hostel area.
Students/hosteller should pay monthly free in advance basis. Rupees 5000/- (Five Thousand) will be charged as a admission fee.
If the student does not stay in the hostel for more than 15 consecutive days (while on leave) the monthly fee will be reduced 10% on the day of confirmation and this facility will be available only once a year.
The mess timings are as follows and the students should strictly adhere to these timings: Breakfast : 6.30 a.m. to 7.30 a.m. Lunch : 9.00 noon to 10.30 a.m. Snacks : 2.00 p.m. to 3.30 p.m. Dinner : 7.00 p.m. to 8.30 p.m.
The hostel operator will not be held accountable in the event of any untoward incident like suicide, disappearance while leaving the hostel, loss of contact, student marriage etc.
Students must fill out an admission form in the presence of a parent. When filling the form, it is mandatory to attach a photocopy of Citizenship / License / ID-Card with photo and introduction.
The boarders will have to strictly adhere to rules and instructions of the Hostel.
Hostel leave will be from Dashain to Chhath. Between Dashain and Tihar, there will be mutual understanding between students and hostel management for college institutes. But hostel management administration will not be obliged to operate hostels.
